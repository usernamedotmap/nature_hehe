Wind of Nature

Wind of Nature is a really good item against high burst physical damage heroes since you will become immune to all physical damage for 2 seconds. This item is only suitable for Marksman Icon marksmen because the duration will be reduced to half when used by a non-Marksman Icon marksmen. It can also provides some attack speed and lifesteal to increase their sustainability when dealing high damage.
